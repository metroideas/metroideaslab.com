Our story

The Metro Ideas Lab is a collaborative space where governments and communities solve public sector challenges together. Located in the newly restored Wheelhouse building on East Main Street, we are launching a new home for civic innovators, academics and engaged residents.

This collaboration starts with the tenants. Metro Ideas Project, a public-benefit research organization and think tank, will make the lab its headquarters. Plus Coffee is opening a satellite shop here to serve nearby workers and residents. Metro Ideas Lab will host policy-focused events, conversations and meetings for residents who want to build a stronger city in 2017.
